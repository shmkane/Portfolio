GradeSnap!

Grade Snap is an integrated learning system that bridges the gap between traditional learning media and emerging technologies.

Students can continue to use traditional learning materials such as paper worksheets and maniplulatives, while receiving digital feedback in real-time.

Teachers use intuitive technology tools to increase grading efficiency and receive actionable insights on student performance

International Space Station Live Tracker

Retrieves the The International Space Station's latitude and longitude in low Earth's orbit via ISS-NOW json API.

Its coordinates are then plotted on a map and updated every second, drawing a line of it's path.

Furthermore, the tracker will let you know when the ISS is flying close to your location!

Python3, JSON
